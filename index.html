Tim Ferriss

“It's lonely at the top. Ninety-nine percent of people in the world are convinced they are incapable of achieving great things, so they aim for the mediocre.”

“If you are insecure, guess what? The rest of the world is, too. Do not overestimate the competition and underestimate yourself. You are better than you think.”

Expert on multiple fronts

Podcaster

Tim hosts the number one business podcast on iTunes. He talks to top performers and dissects their routines, methods and stories for tools you can use right now to improve your life.

Writer

Tim has written three best-selling books. The 4-Hour Workweek helped start life-style design, The 4-Hour Body offered tools to transform your body & The 4-Hour Chef detailed how to learn any skill.

Educator

Tim describes himself as a human guinea pig who tests products and theories on himself so you don't have to. Through his body of work he has improved the lives of thousands of people.

Timeline of Achievements

2005

Becomes first American to hold a Guinness World Record in tango for the most consecutive tango spins in 1 minute.

2010

The Tim Ferriss blog rated #1 of the 150 best management and leadership blogs based on visitors and performance.

2007

The 4-Hour Workweek published. The book has sold over 1.3 million copies and spent nearly 4 years as an NYT bestseller.

2012

The 4-Hour Chef released by Amazon publishing. It reached #1 on Wall Street Journal bestseller list.

2008

Learns Yabusame, the Japanese art of horse archery, for short-lived TV-pilot Trial by Fire.

2013

TV show The Tim Ferriss Experiment debuts. It is currently available on Vimeo.

2010

The 4-Hour Body published. Debuts as no. 1 on the NYT bestseller list.

2014

The Tim Ferriss Show podcast begins. In 2016 the podcast has reached 70 million downloads.
